Knot Just Locs is a full-stack software application designed to simplify the hair appointment experience through a seamless and user-friendly platform. The application allows users to securely create accounts, log in, book hair appointments, and connect with professional stylists by selecting their preferred date, time, and hair specialist through an integrated scheduling system. In addition to appointment booking, users can access personalized hair care advice, styling recommendations, and resources to help maintain healthy hair and locs.
